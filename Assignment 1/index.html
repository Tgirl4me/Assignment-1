WHY DID YOU JOIN ALTSCHOOL SCHOOL OF ENGINEERING PROGRAM?

However, the main reason I joined Altschool is because the tutors and the head of the school are Nigerian. Being a Nigerian in the diaspora, we don't have a lot of options for learning here.

There are people from different nationalities who teach these courses but their accents are difficult to understand, mode of delivery lacking, or the course prices ridiculously expensive so it was a no-brainer to join this school.
